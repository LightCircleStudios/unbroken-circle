Unbroken Circle

In a world where nothing is allowed to fall apart, we keep going… pretending everything is fine. Something inside keeps us standing in society — smiling, surviving, existing. But nothing lasts forever. One moment… one choice… one truth… and the circle shatters. Will you stay unbroken? Or will you finally feel what you've been hiding?
